Project: Cytokine & Secreted protein spatial Isoform distribution inference in the Tumor MicroEnvironment

Executive Summary

This report presents spatial isoform analysis results from SPLISOSM on glioma data using both Visium ONT (11 samples) and Visium SR (13 samples), with comprehensive validation using an independent scRNA-seq dataset (32,304 cells, 16 cell states) and SpaCET spot deconvolution. Using the HSIC-IR (Isoform Ratio) test, analysis of 26 SecAct target genes across 24 total samples identified consistent spatial isoform patterns. Eight genes were validated in scRNA-seq with significant differential splicing (|ΔPSI| > 10%, padj < 0.05), including VCAN with 99% isoform switching between macrophages and tumor cells. SpaCET deconvolution confirms that VCAN isoform ratios correlate positively with tumor content (r=+0.19) and negatively with macrophage content (r=-0.17) at the spot level.

  3. Functional implications: Different VCAN isoforms may have distinct ECM-remodeling and signaling properties in tumor vs immune contexts

10. Methods Summary

Visium ONT Analysis (Discovery)

  • Tool: SPLISOSM (Spatial Isoform Statistical Omnibus Model)
  • Primary Statistic: HSIC-IR (Hilbert-Schmidt Independence Criterion for Isoform Ratio)
  • Kernel: Full-rank ICAR spatial kernel (k=15 neighbors, ρ=0.99)
  • P-value: Liu's chi-squared moment matching
  • Targets: 26 SecAct genes with 2+ isoforms detected
  • Significance threshold: HSIC-IR p-value < 0.05

scRNA-seq Analysis (Validation)

  • Tool: MARVEL (single-cell splicing)
  • Metric: PSI (Percent Spliced In)
  • Events: SE, MXE, A5SS, A3SS, AFE, ALE
  • Statistical test: Wilcoxon rank-sum between tumor states

SpaCET Deconvolution (Cell Type Validation)

  • Tool: SpaCET v1.0 (Spatial Cellular Estimator for Tumors)
  • Cancer type: GBM signature
  • Cell types: 14 major lineage types
  • Correlation: Spearman's rho between cell proportion and isoform fraction
  • Samples: 13 Visium SR glioma samples
