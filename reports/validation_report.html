This report presents spatial isoform analysis results from SPLISOSM on Visium ONT glioma data, with validation using an independent scRNA-seq dataset (32,304 cells). Using the HSIC-IR (Isoform Ratio) test, analysis of 26 SecAct target genes across 11 ONT samples identified 20 genes with significant spatial isoform variation. Two genes (APP, VCAN) were successfully validated in scRNA-seq.

Biological Interpretation

APP isoforms:

  • APP751/770 (exons 7/8 included) - Non-neural, contains KPI (Kunitz Protease Inhibitor) domain

Spatial implication: Regions enriched for neural progenitor-like tumor cells (NPC state) will have lower APP exon inclusion, while mesenchymal-rich regions will have higher inclusion. This creates detectable spatial isoform gradients detected by HSIC-IR.

Key Conclusions

  1. HSIC-IR detects true isoform switching - independent of expression changes, identifying genes with spatial splicing variation.
  2. APP and VCAN isoform variation is cell-state-dependent - different tumor states have distinct splicing programs that create spatial gradients in tissue.
  3. CLU and B2M show universal spatial variation - significant by HSIC-IR in all 11 samples, suggesting fundamental spatial regulation in glioma TME.
  4. Immune-related genes prominent - B2M, CD74, MIF, LGALS1 all show strong HSIC-IR patterns, suggesting spatial immune niche regulation.
  5. Novel isoforms detected by ONT (e.g., CLU truncated forms) may require alternative validation strategies beyond SMART-seq2.

8. Methods Summary

Visium ONT Analysis (Discovery)

  • Tool: SPLISOSM (Spatial Isoform Statistical Omnibus Model)
  • Primary Statistic: HSIC-IR (Hilbert-Schmidt Independence Criterion for Isoform Ratio)
  • Kernel: Full-rank ICAR spatial kernel (k=15 neighbors, ρ=0.99)
  • P-value: Liu's chi-squared moment matching
  • Targets: 26 SecAct genes with 2+ isoforms detected
  • Significance threshold: HSIC-IR p-value < 0.05

scRNA-seq Analysis (Validation)

  • Tool: MARVEL (single-cell splicing)
  • Metric: PSI (Percent Spliced In)
  • Events: SE, MXE, A5SS, A3SS, AFE, ALE
  • Statistical test: Wilcoxon rank-sum between tumor states
